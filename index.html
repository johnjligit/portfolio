SRF Testing Facility Structural Analysis

Research

Advancing materials science.

Composites · NASA Space Grant

Compressive Failure of Woven FRP Composites Under Open Hole Compression

Investigating how hole size affects compressive strength in woven fiber-reinforced polymer laminates used in aerospace, automotive, and naval applications. ASTM D6484 tests on hole sizes of 3–6 mm reveal fracture process zone (FPZ) effects that plateau strength beyond ~6 mm. Sub-scale specimen failures indicate load-introduction-limited behavior requiring further investigation.

Cryogenics · BNL / EIC

Thermal Contact Resistance of RF Spring Fingers at Cryogenic Temperatures

Characterizing the thermal contact resistance and mechanical force of RF spring fingers designed for the Electron Ion Collider beamline. These components operate at temperatures as low as 4 Kelvin, making accurate thermal modeling critical for system performance. Developed custom test fixtures, strain gauge rigs, and Arduino-based data acquisition systems.
